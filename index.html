Web Devs of the Shire

About us

Far beyond the misty mountains, in a quiet corner of the South East, there is a land of silicon and startups... The Thames Valley is home to a huge and growing community of web developers and tech startups.

WDOTS is here to bring together innovative developers, heroic testers and pioneering founders on a quest to make the world a better place.

We host monthly events ranging from lightning talks and presentations to hacks and project inceptions.

So join us for an evening of discussion, code and the occasional moment of brilliance over a mead and a bit of pipe weed once every month in and around Gerrards Cross, Buckinghamshire.
